About

We are a team of students at Interlake High School in Bellevue, Washington that competes in the national Science Olympiad competition.

Our club members explore topics in science, technology, engineering, and math outside of regular school curriculum by studying a wide range of topics, engineering devices, and practicing lab techniques to prepare for competitions against high schools from all over the state. By joining an Interlake Science Olympiad team, our members are able to create a knowledge base that will prepare them for careers in STEM fields.

Our teams compete in Washington state at the Northwest Regional Tournament, and this year is our fourth consecutive year qualifying for the state competition, competing as one of the top 20 teams in the state.

News

April 18, 2015

Interlake performs well at their final tournament of the year, the Washington State Tournament at Highline College in Des Moines, WA, with our top team winning third place and our second team winning sixth place, breaking records in overall and individual performance! Our teams medalled in 20 events (breaking the previous record of 15 and a huge improvement from the 11 the previous year). We are extremely grateful for all the support from our coaches, school, parents, and sponsors this year and are excited for the 2016 season!

March 07, 2015

Interlake performs well again at their second tournament of the year, the Northwest Regional Tournament at Highline College in Des Moines, WA, with our top team taking second place out of 22 other teams! Our second team won fifth place, which means that both teams qualify to move on to the State Tournament on April 18, also at Highline College.

February 07, 2015

Interlake High School starts off on the right foot at their first tournament of the year, winning the first place trophy at the Raisbeck Aviation High School Invitational, with many of our 15 members earning medals in their individual events!

December 06, 2015

After over a month of planning, Interlake hosted its inaugural Science Olympiad invitational tournament, with 28 teams from schools throughout Washington competing. Congratulations to Bothell High School, our tournament's champions!
