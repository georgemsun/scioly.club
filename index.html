Interlake Science Olympiad

About

We are a club at Interlake High School that competes in the national Science Olympiad competition. Our club members explore topics in science, technology, engineering, and math outside of regular school curriculum by studying a wide range of topics, engineering devices, and practicing lab techniques to prepare for competitions against high schools from all over the state. By joining an Interlake Science Olympiad team, our members are able to create a knowledge base that will prepare them for careers in STEM fields.

Our teams compete in Washington state at the Northwest Regional competition, and this year is our fourth year qualifying for the state competition, competing as one of the top 20 teams in the state.
