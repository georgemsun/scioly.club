Positions

  • There will be a total of 7 officers.
  • There will be a maximum of 4 senior officers.
  • The two most voted-for candidates will be named President and Vice President. The next 5 most voted-for candidates will become officers, and will be delegated a position by the 2015-2016 officers.
  • The delegated positions will be: Treasurer, Secretary, Newcomer Liaison, and two Build Captains.

Running

  • Applications for candidacy must be emailed to interlakescienceolympiad@gmail.com by Tuesday April 28 11:59 PM.
  • The current officers will announce the names of all candidates on April 29, 2015.
  • Each candidate will give a speech up to 3 minutes long on May 01.

Voting

  • The election is on Friday, May 01, 2015.
  • Only those who went to Invitationals, Regionals and/or State tournaments can vote.
  • YOUR VOTE:
    • You have a maximum of 7 votes, and you may choose to use fewer than 7 votes. You can choose to vote for any combination of candidates no matter their grade level.
    • In your vote, you will also rank your preference for one candidate over another, which will be further explained at elections. This will only be used in the event of a tie; otherwise, all votes have equal weight.
    • All pertinent information included above will be repeated during elections.
  • EMAIL VOTING:
    • You must first email interlakescienceolympiad@gmail.com two things: 1) a notice you will email vote, and 2) a reasonable excuse for not coming to the election. This must be done before Wednesday, April 29.
    • You will then be emailed instructions on how to vote and blurbs (2-3 sentence platform statements) from all of the candidates on April 29.
    • You will lastly be able to email your vote between April 29 and May 01.
